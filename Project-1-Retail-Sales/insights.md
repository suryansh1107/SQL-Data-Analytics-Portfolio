# 🔍 Business Insights — Retail Sales Analysis

## Executive Summary

Retail store analysis covering 7 months (Jan–Jul 2024), 10 customers, 12 products, 30 transactions totaling ₹5,29,400 in revenue.

---

## 📈 Revenue Insights

### Monthly Performance
| Month | Revenue | Growth % | Status |
|-------|---------|----------|--------|
| Jan 2024 | ₹87,000 | — | Baseline |
| Feb 2024 | ₹88,600 | +1.8% | Stable |
| Mar 2024 | ₹34,400 | -61.2% | ⚠️ CRASH |
| Apr 2024 | ₹62,700 | +82.3% | Recovery |
| May 2024 | ₹91,000 | +45.1% | 🏆 BEST |
| Jun 2024 | ₹77,100 | -15.3% | Slight dip |
| Jul 2024 | ₹88,600 | +14.9% | Strong |

### Key Observation
March 2024 saw a -61.2% crash — only ₹34,400 revenue with 5 orders. No high-value (Laptop/Smartphone) purchase in this month. Action needed: Investigate why premium product sales dropped in March.

---

## 📦 Product Insights

### Revenue Concentration Risk
- Top 2 products (Laptop + Smartphone) = 85.9% of revenue
- If either product goes out of stock, business suffers severely
- Recommendation: Diversify product mix, push Clothing and Sports categories

### Underperforming Categories
- Books: ₹4,800 (0.9%) — Very low
- Food: ₹4,500 (0.85%) — Very low
- Consider: Bundle deals, discounts, or discontinue
